the Nazca lines are believed to be created between 500BC and 500AD and during this time there was consider to be 2 phases of the Nazca lines

  1. Paracas Phase: believed to spaning from 400-200BC
  2. Nazca Phase: second phase of constructed lines in 200BC- 500AD

between 80-100 new figures have been found in recent years as close as 2020 through the use of drones, this new figures are found to be designed in the shape of animals and plants such as trees flowers and as of 2020 a cat design was found on a hilltop with archaeologists believing there may be many more yet to be discovered
